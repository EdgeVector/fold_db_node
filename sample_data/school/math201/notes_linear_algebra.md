# Math 201 - Linear Algebra Notes

## Chapter 3: Vector Spaces

### Definition
A **vector space** V over a field F is a set with two operations:
- Vector addition: V x V -> V
- Scalar multiplication: F x V -> V

### Key Properties
1. Closure under addition and scalar multiplication
2. Associativity and commutativity of addition
3. Existence of zero vector and additive inverses
4. Distributivity of scalar multiplication

### Subspaces
A subset W of V is a subspace if:
- 0 is in W
- W is closed under addition
- W is closed under scalar multiplication

### Basis and Dimension
- A **basis** is a linearly independent spanning set
- The **dimension** of V is the number of vectors in any basis
- Example: R^3 has dimension 3, standard basis {e1, e2, e3}
